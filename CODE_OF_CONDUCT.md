# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

Contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Keep feedback focused on the work
- Welcome new contributors
- Avoid harassment, insults, and personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Threats or intimidation
- Discriminatory language
- Personal attacks
- Sharing private information without permission
- Repeated disruption of project discussion

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project community.
